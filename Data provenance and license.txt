Provenance:
The data used ("Country and Territory Ratings and Statuses, 1973-2020") and other Freedom House data can be downloaded from: https://web.archive.org/web/20201209152930/https://freedomhouse.org/report/freedom-world
Line 41 in the script also downloads it, and the data is also available in this repository with the name "FH_raw".

License:
The usage permissions for Freedom House data are available here: https://freedomhouse.org/about-us/content-permissions
Permission to use Freedom House data is only needed if it is for commercial use. 
If the intention is non-commercial and Fair Use (e.g. teaching and research), and Freedom House is clearly cited, no permission is needed to use and share their data online).
